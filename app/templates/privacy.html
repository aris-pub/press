Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15.3.2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Scroll Press. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our preprint server platform.

This policy complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applies to all users of Scroll Press.

2. Data Controller

Leo Torres
Emilienstrasse 19

Germany
Email: leo@leotrs.com

3. What Data We Collect

3.1 Account Information

  • Email address
  • Name

3.2 Content Data

  • Uploaded manuscripts and preprints
  • Metadata (title, abstract, authors, keywords)
  • Version history
  • Comments and discussions

3.3 Technical Data

  • IP address (temporarily for rate limiting and security purposes)
  • Browser type and version
  • Date and time of access
  • Pages visited

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR): To provide and maintain our preprint hosting service
  • Contract performance (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR): To fulfill our service agreement with you
  • Legal obligations (Article 6(1)(c) GDPR): To comply with applicable laws

5. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide preprint hosting services
  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your preprints with proper attribution
  • Enable commenting and academic discourse
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security
  • Communicate important service updates

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell or rent your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Public: Preprints and associated metadata are publicly accessible by design
  • Subprocessors: Third-party service providers that process data on our behalf (see Section 6.1 below)
  • Legal authorities: Only when required by law

6.1 Subprocessors (Data Processors)

We use the following third-party service providers to operate Scroll Press. Each operates under a data processing agreement:

Provider Purpose Data processed Location
Supabase Database and data storage Account data, manuscript content, metadata EU (Frankfurt)
Fly.io Application hosting All data transmitted through the platform EU (Amsterdam)
Resend Transactional email delivery Email addresses, email content US
Sentry Error tracking and monitoring Technical data, IP addresses, error context US

We will update this list if we add or change subprocessors.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Preprints: Retained indefinitely as part of the scholarly record, even after account deletion or retraction
  • Retracted preprints: Remain publicly accessible with a retraction notice to preserve the scholarly record and maintain citation integrity
  • Technical logs: Deleted after 30 days

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your personal data (except published preprints, which remain for scholarly integrity)
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at leo@leotrs.com.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls
  • Secure data storage

10. Cookies

We use only essential cookies for session management. These cookies are necessary for the platform to function and cannot be disabled. We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies.

11. International Transfers

Your primary data (account information and manuscripts) is stored within the European Union. Some subprocessors (Resend, Sentry) are based in the United States and may process limited data outside the EU. These transfers are protected by the EU-US Data Privacy Framework and standard contractual clauses.

12. Children's Privacy

Scroll Press is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform announcement.

14. Complaints

If you have concerns about how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

15. Contact Information

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact:

Leo Torres
Email: leo@leotrs.com

For questions about these terms or our policies, please contact us.
