About Scroll Press

Research publications. Web-native. Human-first.

Scroll Press is where modern research lives. We host HTML-native preprints that work beautifully on every device - from your phone on the train to your desktop in the lab.

No more squinting at PDFs. No more pinch-to-zoom. Just research that respects your time and works everywhere.

Open and Transparent

All content on Scroll Press is freely accessible. We believe research should be open to everyone, from students to seasoned researchers, regardless of institutional affiliations or subscription access.

Built for the Future

Scroll Press is part of The Aris Program, building the infrastructure for post-PDF scientific publishing. We're working toward a future where research is interactive, accessible, and truly digital-first.
